miracidium of Megalodiscus temperatus stained with Nile blue sulfate.  The apical gland (AG), nucleus of the apical gland (NA), penetration gland (PG), brain or ganglion (BR), nucleus of a cell of the brain (NB), secretory sac (SS), one of the nuclei of the secretory sac (NS), germ ball (GB) and cilia (CI) can be seen.  The organism is syncytial and cell membranes do not separate adjacent cells.  Each of the above organs listed are multinucleate.  1000x
