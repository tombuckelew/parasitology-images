Trypanosoma lewisi is a non-pathogenic trypanosome infecting rats.  This is a cross section through the midbody, which includes the large spheroidal body, the chrondriome, some ribosomal endoplasmic reticulum and the subpellicular microtubules.  The body extending off to the right is the undulating membrane with a distinct 9+2 microtubular arrangement.  70,000x
